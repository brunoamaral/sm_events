About

The other day +vasco vasconcelos asked if I knew about upcoming events around social media / communication this year in Lisbon. Truth is, I have been so far away from events I didn't know what to tell him.

Instead of asking around and send him an email I had another idea. Why not #crowdsource a list of social media events ?

A few minutes later I had a working version of this app, using a google form and some great javascript magic by Jessica Lord.

There is a small bug that prevents people logged off from Google from seeing the table of events. I'll apply a fix as soon as I can find the time. In the meantime, if you see a blank page, login to gmail and go back to it. :) ï»¿
